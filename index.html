Core Intelligence Modules

Six Modules. One Complete Compliance Intelligence Platform.

SpecDriftGS combines multi-channel document ingestion, structured extraction, semantic drift detection, blast-radius mapping, risk scoring, and workflow automation all in one unified AI-native SaaS platform.

Module 01

Multi-Channel Document Ingestion

Receive supplier evidence through email forwarding, manual upload, supplier portal, or API connectors wherever documents arrive.

The ingestion layer captures supplier revisions, allergen statements, change notices, and certificates through all channels your team already uses. Email forwarding routes inbox attachments automatically. The supplier portal gives external parties a controlled upload environment. API connectors enable future integration with upstream systems ensuring no critical update gets trapped in an email chain.
Module 02

Semantic Drift Detection Engine

Goes beyond version tracking determines whether a change is materially meaningful from a compliance perspective.

When a supplier changes "rice flour" to "wheat flour," most systems see edited text. SpecDriftGS sees a new declarable allergen. When a certificate is renewed with narrower scope, we identify a potential loss of approval support. This domain-aware interpretation is the core innovation AI extraction combined with structured normalisation and explainable compliance reasoning.
Module 03

Blast-Radius Graph Mapping

Maps every downstream consequence of an upstream change from raw materials to finished SKUs, labels, claims, and customer documents.

The blast-radius model is SpecDriftGS's most powerful differentiator. Rather than stopping at "something changed," the platform links one upstream change to its full network of dependent records. Which SKUs are affected? Which labels need review? Which customer submissions are now at risk? The platform answers all four critical questions: what changed, why it matters, what else is exposed, and who needs to act.

How It Works

From Fragmented Supplier Updates
to Structured Compliance Intelligence

SpecDriftGS operates as a closed-loop compliance intelligence lifecycle: detect change → interpret meaning → map impact → trigger action → preserve evidence. Every step is designed to convert incoming supplier documents into controlled, auditable compliance decisions not just filed records.

The 6-Phase Compliance Intelligence Lifecycle

A Complete Operational Loop Not Just a Dashboard

Phase 01 · Ingest

Capture Every Supplier Document Update

SpecDriftGS ingests supplier evidence through all channels email forwarding, manual upload, supplier portal, and API connectors. Specifications, allergen statements, certificates, packaging declarations, and change notices are all captured automatically.

No supplier update gets trapped in an email inbox or a local file folder. The platform creates a structured record of every incoming document from the moment of receipt.

Email Forwarding Supplier Portal API Connectors
Phase 02 · Extract

Convert Documents Into Structured Compliance Data

Once received, the platform converts unstructured supplier PDFs into normalised compliance data. Allergen fields, certificate scope attributes, claim statements, ingredient lists, and packaging declarations are all structured for downstream analysis.

This extraction layer is critical downstream drift detection depends entirely on the accuracy and consistency of how supplier evidence is interpreted and recorded.

Phase 03 · Detect

Identify Meaningful Change Not Just File Versions

Semantic drift detection compares structured data from new and previous documents to determine whether a change is materially meaningful. "Rice flour" changed to "wheat flour" is not just edited text it is a new declarable allergen. A renewed certificate with narrower scope is not just a new file it may invalidate an existing approval.

Allergen Changes Certificate Scope Drift Packaging Declaration
Phase 04 · Map

Blast-Radius: Who and What Is Affected?

The blast-radius graph maps every downstream consequence of one upstream change. Which finished SKUs rely on this ingredient? Which labels carry this claim? Which customer submissions reference this certificate? Which markets are at risk?

The platform answers all four critical questions: what changed, why it matters, what else is now exposed, and who needs to act.

Phase 05 · Act

Structured Corrective Action Workflows

Risk-prioritised findings are routed into structured corrective action workflows. Critical allergen alerts are escalated immediately. Certificate scope issues are held for technical review. Lower-severity packaging drift is queued for scheduled review.

Every action is logged, time-stamped, and linked to the source evidence ensuring traceability throughout the remediation process.

Auto-Escalation Structured Review Workflow Closure
Phase 06 · Evidence

Audit-Ready Evidence at Every Step

Every decision is supported by evidence, traceability, and a clear record of review, escalation, and closure. Compliance managers can generate audit-ready documentation at any time showing exactly what was detected, when, by what logic, and what action was taken.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SpecDriftGS and what it means for your compliance operations in the AI-native era.

What is SpecDriftGS and why does my food business need it?

+
SpecDriftGS is an AI-native compliance intelligence platform designed to prevent silent compliance failures in regulated product businesses. It continuously monitors supplier specifications, allergen statements, and certificates detecting when upstream document changes create downstream risk for your labels, allergen declarations, product claims, and release decisions. Unlike generic document management, it determines whether a change is materially meaningful, maps the affected records, and triggers a structured corrective action workflow with full audit traceability.

How is this different from our existing specification management or QMS system?

+
Existing systems typically store documents, manage approvals, or route quality workflows. They can tell you that a file changed but they cannot determine whether that change affects an allergen declaration, invalidates a product claim, narrows a certification scope, or creates a release risk. SpecDriftGS is specifically designed to answer those questions through semantic drift detection, domain-aware AI interpretation, and blast-radius impact mapping. It sits alongside your existing systems as a specialised intelligence layer, not a replacement.

What types of documents does SpecDriftGS monitor?

+
The platform monitors supplier specifications, allergen statements, certificates of conformance, packaging declarations, change notices, and any other supplier-side documentation that could affect your downstream compliance records. It is designed to handle the fragmented, semi-structured documents that regulated food businesses actually receive in practice not just clean, standardised data feeds.

What is "blast-radius mapping" and why does it matter?

+
Blast-radius mapping is SpecDriftGS's most distinctive capability. Rather than simply flagging that a supplier document changed, the platform maps all downstream records that depend on that document finished SKUs, labels, customer specifications, claims, approval statuses, and release decisions. This means your team immediately knows not just that something changed, but exactly what is now at risk and who needs to act. Most adjacent systems operate inside a single process boundary and cannot infer these cross-record consequences.

Is SpecDriftGS compliant with UK food safety and data regulations?

+
Yes. SpecDriftGS is designed to comply with UK GDPR and ICO guidance on AI and data protection. The platform supports compliance with the Food Standards Agency's allergen guidance and the UK's food labelling regulations. All AI reasoning is designed to be explainable and human-in-the-loop the platform supports your compliance decisions, it does not make autonomous regulatory determinations on your behalf.

Which sectors does SpecDriftGS serve?

+
Initially, SpecDriftGS is focused on UK food and drink manufacturers particularly SMEs with real compliance obligations but limited internal automation capacity. The platform is then planned to expand into importers of prepacked foods, supplements and nutraceuticals, cosmetics and personal care, medical nutrition products, and selected pharma-adjacent quality environments where supplier documentation, specification drift, and traceability are commercially critical.

How do I get started with SpecDriftGS?

+
Getting started is straightforward. We begin with a scoping session to understand your supplier document flows, compliance obligations, and existing systems. The platform connects to your incoming document channels through email forwarding or API integration, and we configure the blast-radius model around your product records. Most customers are running live compliance monitoring within days, not months. Book a demo to discuss your specific situation.
